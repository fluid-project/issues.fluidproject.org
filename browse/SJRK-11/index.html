    Description

    The "Listen to my story" buttons now no longer read the story out properly. This is likely due to switching from fluid.stringTemplate to gpii-handlebars, so that the message_readStoryText value no longer resolves properly at runtime.

    It currently resolves to ", by ." in English, so the story title, author name and story content are not being substituted properly.
