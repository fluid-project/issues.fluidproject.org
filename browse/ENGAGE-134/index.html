    Description

    Mockup images, used to show unimplemented features, needed to have 1px padding, or will break the cabinet

    If the padding wasn't added the cabinet would initialize but you wouldn't be able to open the drawer to show the full image. It would display only a thin sliver of the image and would close immediately after being openned.
