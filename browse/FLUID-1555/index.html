    Description

    Focus styling not applied to movable portlets on mouse click

    Steps to reproduce:

    1) Open the the uPortal example from the daily build site:
    http://build.fluidproject.org/fluid/sample-code/reorderer/portal/portal.html

    2) Use the mouse to click on the title bar of any of the movable portlets. Notice that no focus styling is applied

    Note that if you click on the locked portlet, the focus styling will be applied to it.

    Environments

    FF2, FF3, Opera 9.5, Safari 3 (Mac OS 10.5)
    FF2, FF3, Opera 9.5 (Win XP)
    FF3 (Win Vista)
    FF3 (Win 2000)
