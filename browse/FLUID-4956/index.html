    • Jonathan Hung commented 2013-04-01T10:44:39.429-0400

      This was being caused by Apple's system preferences of showing scrollbars always.
      To fix, go to:
      1. System preferences > General > Show scroll bars
      2. Set to "When Scroll" or "Automatic"
