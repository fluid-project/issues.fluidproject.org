    • Arash Sadr commented 2013-03-28T15:00:10.688-0400

      We decided to come up with something simple and easy to use. A floating control panel appears when text to speech is turned on. It has play/pause and stop button, and a separated section as a header, indicating that it could be dragged.
      If user presses play, text to speech will automatically start reading the content of the page, which could be tabbed through. The user could also highlight something (a paragraph) and press Play. By pressing Pause, text to speech will stop and stay where it was paused. But pressing Stop will jump to the beginning of the selected paragraph or page.
