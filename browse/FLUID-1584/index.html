    Description

    Mouse over inline edit example 2 - customized edit field, when placing mouse over the 's' of the bold text in the word 'dogs' the the inline editable text flashes between highlighted and not highlighted.

    Steps to reproduce:

    1. Open url: http://build.fluidproject.org/fluid/fluid-components/html/InlineEdit.html

    2. Mouse over inline edit example 2 on the 's' of the bolded word 'dogs.

    3. The inline editable text flashes between being highlighted and not highlighted.

    Environments
