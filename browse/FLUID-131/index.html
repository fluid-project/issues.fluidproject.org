    Description

    If you open up a Gallery and leave it idle until the session times out, you can still reorder items on the page. The page will show the items reordering appearing to work, but really nothing is being saved to the back-end, and when you refresh, re-sign-in and go to check your gallery everything will be back as it was.

    Steps to reproduce:

    • Open Gallery
    • Let it sit idle for 30 minutes.
    • Move elements on the page

    Observe that Lightbox doesn't produce any feedback that the session has timed out.

    Environments

    FF2, FF3, Opera 9.5, Safari 3 (Mac OS 10.5)
    FF2, FF3, IE6, IE7, Opera 9.5 (Win XP)
    FF3, IE7 (Win Vista)
    Sakai 2.5.x
