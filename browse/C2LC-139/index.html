    Description

    Ability to see/hear/feel an immediate reaction to the program the student is building to keep them engaged in the activity

    Description from co-design:

    • Most students had difficulty connecting what’s happening on the projector to what was being projected on the screen
    • Most students need hints/assistance to be able to detect cause and effect relationships.
    • Direct connection between cause and effect was helpful. So students were more focused on the activity

    We could do low-fi design and dev prototypes
