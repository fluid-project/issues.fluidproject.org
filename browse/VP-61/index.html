    Description

    Transcripts disappear when enabled on Windows, and never appear on Mac.

    Windows:

    • Load http://build.fluidproject.org/videoPlayer/videoPlayer/demos/VideoPlayer.html
    • Click full screen
    • Show transcript
    • transcript appears overlapping the video
    • play the video
    • transcript disappears
    • any subsequent requests to show transcripts does not make it appear in full screen.

    Mac:

    • Load http://build.fluidproject.org/videoPlayer/videoPlayer/demos/VideoPlayer.html
    • Click full screen
    • Show transcript
    • Nothing appears

    Environments

    Firefox 18, Windows, Mac

    • Anastasia Cheetham commented 2013-01-16T14:18:32.251-0500

      It does seem to me that the transcript window is still present, just not visible: it is actually still functional.

      After the transcripts have disappeared,
      1) tab through the controls to where they would have been (i.e. three tabs past the fullscreen button)
      2) arrow down as if you were selecting a later transcript phrase
      3) press enter

      The video will navigate to the timecode appropriate for the transcript phrase you selected.
