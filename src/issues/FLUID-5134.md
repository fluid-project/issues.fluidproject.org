On Internet Explorer, the following Reorderer shortcuts conflict:

1\. Ctrl-J conflicts with IE's View Downloads\
2\. Ctrl-K conflicts with IE's Duplicate Tab \
3\. Ctrl-I conflicts with IE's Open Favourites
